You can quickly exit this website.

This website uses a safety feature to help you quickly hide the page you’re on. It’s designed to be less noticeable to someone around you than closing the web browser completely.

Exit this page button

When you’re on a page with the ‘Exit this page’ button, you can click the button or press the ‘Esc’ button on your keyboard 3 times.

Using ‘Exit this page’ will take you to BBC Weather immediately.

‘Exit this page’ does not actually erase your browser history. If you think someone is monitoring what you do online, you should take other precautions to stay safe
